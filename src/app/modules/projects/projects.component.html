Postal Highway- Upgrading of Jamadah (MRM Golbajar-Lagadi-Gadayani Road CH 15+000 - 27+467 (HPR/337011284/076-77/UR-011)

Quick Construction Pvt. Ltd. signed a postal highway road construction project of Golbajar-Ladagi-Gadhyani Road of Siraha District as a sub-contract from main contractor of this project Contech Pvt. Ltd. on 2021 November Under mutual terms and conditions. This project is located at Siraha District, of Madesh Pradesh ( Province), Nepal, which connect Mahendra raj marg to south India border crossing east-west postal highway(hulaki rajmarg). Total project is of about 27.456 km which is divided into two project - project 10 & project 11. Quick Construction singed for project two which 12.456 km which starts from Ramjanki Chowk of Hakpara, Siraha and ends at border of India. This road is 12 meter width with Asphalt construction. This is also one of the national highway road.
